AI Usage Tracker

Get help with AI Usage Tracker for Safari

Getting Started

  1. Open Safari on your Mac
  2. Go to Safari → Settings → Extensions
  3. Enable AI Usage Tracker
  4. Grant permissions for the AI platform websites you use
  5. Click the extension icon in Safari's toolbar to view your dashboard

Frequently Asked Questions

Which AI platforms are supported?

AI Usage Tracker currently supports ChatGPT (chat.openai.com & chatgpt.com), Claude (claude.ai), Gemini (gemini.google.com), and Perplexity (perplexity.ai).

Is my data sent anywhere?

No. All data is stored locally on your Mac using Safari's extension storage. Nothing is sent to any server. Your usage data never leaves your device.

How do I export my data?

Click the extension icon in Safari, then click the Settings gear icon. In the settings panel, click "Export Data" to download a JSON file of your usage history.

How do I reset my data?

Open the extension settings panel and click "Reset All Data". This will permanently delete all tracked usage data. This action cannot be undone.

Why isn't the extension tracking my usage?

Make sure tracking is enabled in the extension settings. Also verify that the extension has permissions for the AI platform websites in Safari → Settings → Extensions → AI Usage Tracker.

How long is my data kept?

Usage data is automatically cleaned up after 90 days. You can also manually reset data at any time from the settings panel.

What does the idle timeout setting do?

The idle timeout determines how long (in seconds) the extension waits without detecting activity before pausing the time tracker. Default is 60 seconds. You can adjust this from 10 to 300 seconds.
