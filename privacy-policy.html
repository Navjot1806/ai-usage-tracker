Privacy Policy

AI Usage Tracker

Last updated: February 8, 2026

Overview

AI Usage Tracker is a Safari Web Extension that helps you monitor your usage of AI platforms including ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection

AI Usage Tracker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All tracking data is stored entirely on your device using the browser's local storage API.

The extension tracks the following metrics locally:

  • Number of messages sent to AI platforms
  • Time spent on AI platform websites
  • Session counts per platform

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using the Safari extension storage API. Specifically:

  • Data is stored in browser local storage only
  • No data is sent to any external server
  • No cloud sync or backup of tracking data occurs
  • Data is automatically cleaned up after 90 days

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with third parties. Since all data remains on your device, there is no data to share.

Network Access

The extension does not make any network requests. It operates entirely offline after installation. The only web access required is to the AI platform websites you visit, which is handled by Safari itself, not the extension.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save usage statistics locally on your device
  • Tabs: To detect which AI platform tab is active for time tracking
  • Alarms: To schedule periodic data cleanup
  • Host permissions: Limited to AI platform domains (chat.openai.com, chatgpt.com, claude.ai, gemini.google.com, perplexity.ai) to detect message activity

User Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Disable tracking at any time from the extension settings
  • Export your data as a JSON file for personal review
  • Reset all data to permanently delete all tracked information
  • Uninstall the extension to remove all stored data

Children's Privacy

AI Usage Tracker does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The extension does not collect personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
